Buddi Smart Feeder & Collar

Pets' eating and drinking habits can be some of the first signs of an issue with a pet. The Buddi smart feeder & collar allows dog and cat owners track their pets' activity, food and water intake, set refill reminders, track their location, and record medical information. Buddi allows pet owners to better understand their pets health and provides data and insights into their activity.
