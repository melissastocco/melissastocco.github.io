SWAGUR

SWaGUR is a games user research program at the University of Waterloo and University of Saskatchewan. Marim Ganaba and I designed a Wordpress Website for the program to promote to prospective students.

TEAM: MARIM GANABA, MELISSA STOCCO, LENNART NACKE

My role: For this Website I helped create the site map, I wrote the web copy and aided Marim Ganaba with the overall design. I designed the icon graphics in the "What will you learn?" section of the homepage as well as the blue background image for "Ready for a career in Games User Research" section.
