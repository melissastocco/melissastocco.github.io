Umbra Canvas

Due to the shift to online/remote work many users are spending more time in their homes. Users need their spaces to be more flexible to accommodate their work and home lives. Canvas provides separation and functionality without sacrificing beauty and aesthetics. The room divider offers privacy, light-filtering, display/storage and personalization.
