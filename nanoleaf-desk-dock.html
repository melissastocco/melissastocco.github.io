Nanoleaf Desk Dock

The Pegboard Desk Dock is a place to store gaming controllers and headphones while also acting as an ambient light. With the products unique pegboard light design, controllers and devices are illuminated when stored on the product. Using Nanoleaf’s smart technology the light can screen mirror pc games providing an immersive experience.
