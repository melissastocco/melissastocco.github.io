Ryde Balance Bike

The Ryde balance bike has an eye-catching and playful design. Children can use their imagination and creativity to add chalkboard designs onto the front panel and the wheels of the bike. The form is functional and economical since it allows for various adjustments to the bike to grow with the child. Finally, the design uses natural materials.

TEAM: MELISSA STOCCO, THOMAS PURCHASE, MARY-BETH SCULLY

⇠

ADJUSTABILITY

The handle bars, saddle, and wheelbase are adjustable to allow the bike to grow with the child.

STEERING

The bike is able to steer and uses a pin and hole system with an attached bracket on the front forks.

PERSONALIZATION

The blue chalkboard paint on the front panel and wheels of the bike allows the child to customize their bike.

SUSTAINABILITY

The bike is made of quality materials. The frame of the bike is birch veneered plywood and the handle wraps and seat are made of sustainable leather. The wheels are recycled rubber.
