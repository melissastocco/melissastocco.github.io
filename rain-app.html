Rain - Smart Shower

Rain is a smart shower that keeps the users shower clean by conducting regular automated cleaning cycles. This eliminates the need to manually clean the shower which can be difficult for seniors and users with disabilities. The shower includes other features to reduce water-waste and damage prevention.
