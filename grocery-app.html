Pandemic Grocery Store App

The brief was to design a solution for grocery stores during the pandemic. We first defined our demographics and personas. Then we conducted a survey, user observation and secondary research. We found the problem of waiting outside in line during the height of the pandemic. Finally, we narrowed down to the best solution and combined the best features to design our pandemic application.
