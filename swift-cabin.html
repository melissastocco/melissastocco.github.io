Swift Cabin

The primary focus of this project was to redesign the cabin interior and create four areas where the client, a tier one supplier of automotive plastic components and systems in North America, can manufacture and assemble innovative design solutions for the target user. The goal for this project was to create opportunities that emphasize the client’s plastic content that can be manufactured in this market and further benefit the OEMs.

The focus of this design is surrounding residential and commercial Parcel Delivery Trucks. The solution uses primary and secondary research to guide design decisions, functionality, and accordances.
