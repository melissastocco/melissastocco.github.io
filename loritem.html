Loritem Toiletry Kit

Loritem is a rapidly-renewable and sustainable system of toiletries designed for 4-5 star hotels. Loritem includes a set of plant and bio-based shower and beauty items, as well as a complimentary, multi-pocketed cosmetics bag. This system will solve the issue of disposable plastics for toiletries in hotels, while continuing to provide customers with high quality complimentary items.

TEAM PROJECT

⇠

MISSION

To provide hotel chains and their customers with a low environmental impact solution for disposable toiletry items, while still providing them with high-end complimentary items that will appeal to their customer-base.

IMPACT

Approximately 200 million toiletry bottles are used each year by a single large hotel chain. Loritem eliminates plastic complimentary items intended for single-use purposes. Instead, the provided items are compostable and have a low environmental footprint.

ACCESSIBILITY

Loritem allows hotel guests to easily differentiate products through the use of unique colours. The welcome label includes braille and the pods have divots to differentiate between shampoo and conditioner for the visually impaired.

SHIPPING

Densely packed toiletries while shipping reduces cost and fuel consumption in transport, while allowing hotels to still acquire high quantities of product. A 12 x 10” box fits 210 pod packs or 420 soap bars/sulapac containers. 64 boxes fit on a single 48 x40” pallet.
