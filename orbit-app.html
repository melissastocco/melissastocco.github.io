Orbit Home Gym

Users have busy schedules and cannot always fit fitness or the time to learn how to exercise properly into their daily routine. Orbit provides complete workout routines led by fitness instructors the user can do at home. The solution monitors the workouts through the smart home gym mat. The digital application allows the user to schedule their workouts, pick different types of exercises, abd shows the user difficulty levels of each workout.
